Our Story

Established in 1995, Prasad Jewellers began as a humble family tradition rooted in trust, purity, and craftsmanship.

For nearly three decades, we have specialised in creating traditional gold and silver jewellery that reflects cultural heritage and timeless design.

We believe jewellery is not just an ornament, but a memory passed across generations. That is why we follow a made-to-order approach — every piece is crafted only after you place your order, exactly as per your design preference.

Our journey since 1995 is built on long-term relationships, transparent purity, and the skill of experienced artisans who handcraft every piece with care.
